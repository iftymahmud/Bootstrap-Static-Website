Who We Are

The Great Commission is a command given by Jesus to his disciples before his ascension into heaven. It can be found in the New Testament of the Bible, specifically in Matthew 28:19-20: "Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age." This commission is a call for believers to spread the message of the Gospel and make disciples of all people, baptizing them and teaching them to follow the teachings of Jesus. It is considered a crucial aspect of the Christian faith and a central part of fulfilling the purpose of God's plan for humanity.
